About Me

I am a PhD student at MPI-SWS in the group for Automated Verification and Approximation lead by Eva Darulova. In general I have a strong interest in static analysis and compilers for finite-precision programs. For these tools my main focus is on establishing rigorous correctness proofs for their behaviours.

We are currently working on verified compilation of floating-point programs in the presence of optimizing compilers. So far we have designed a new semantics called Icing that is able to model floating-point optimizations in a verified compiler.

In a second project we worked on building a certification backend for my supervisors previous work in the Daisy framework. To this end we implement a checker for Daisy's results in both Coq and HOL4. The overall goal for the project is to support certification of analysis results computed by Daisy.
